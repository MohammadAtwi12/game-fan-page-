ALL ASSASSIN'S CREED GAMES

Assassin's Creed Unity

In Assassin’s Creed Unity, play as Arno and experience the French Revolution like never before. Storm the Bastille, witness the execution of King Louis XVI, and help the people of France carve an entirely new destiny.

Assassin's Creed Syndicate

In Assassin's Creed Syndicate, the brash and rebellious Jacob Frye takes enemies on with lightning-fast multi kills and countermoves. Use improved stealth tactics to elude enemies and unleash an arsenal of weapons.

Assassin's Creed Origins

Assassin’s Creed Origins uncovers the beginning of the Brotherhood. Fight in epic battles, master a reinvented combat system, and explore Egypt. Complete missions in any order and follow your path to greatness.

Assassin's Creed Valhala

In Assassin’s Creed Valhalla, you are Eivor, a fierce Viking warrior. Explore a dynamic open world set against the brutal backdrop of England’s Dark Ages. Raid your enemies and build your political power in your quest to earn your place in Valhalla.

Assassin's Creed Black Flag

In Assassin’s Creed IV Black Flag, play as Captain Edward Kenway. Stand among legendary pirates like Blackbeard and establish a lawless republic in the Bahamas. Relive the truly explosive events that defined the Golden Age of Pirates.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey

Write an epic odyssey and become a legendary Greek hero in Assassin’s Creed Odyssey, an inspiring adventure where you must forge your destiny. Influence how history unfolds as you discover a rich world shaped by your decisions.

Assassin's Creed Rogue

Assassin's Creed Ⅲ

Assassin’s Creed III takes you back to the American Revolutionary War, a time of civil unrest. A Native American assassin fights to protect his land and people, and he will ignite the flames of a young nation’s revolution.

Assassin's Creed Ⅱ

Assassin’s Creed II is the follow-up to the title that became the fastest-selling new IP in video game history. The highly anticipated title features a new hero, Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a young Italian noble from the Renaissance.

Master Assassin, Ezio Auditore da Firenze, walks in the footsteps of legendary mentor, Altair. The perilous path is one that will take Ezio to Constantinople, where an army of Templars threatens to destabilize the region.

Assassin's Creed Brotherhood

In Assassin’s Creed Brotherhood, live as Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a legendary Master Assassin, as he struggles against the powerful Templar Order. To strike at the heart of the enemy, he must journey into Rome, the center of corruption.
